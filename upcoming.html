The most powerful AMG electric vehicle ever made, with an estimated 1,200 hp. A bespoke aerodynamics package developed on the Nürburgring and a 0–100 Km/h time of under 2 seconds make this a landmark machine.

The legendary naturally-aspirated flat-six meets electrification for the first time. The 2027 GT3 Hybrid uses a front-axle electric motor for additional performance while retaining the iconic analogue character that makes the GT3 a driver's benchmark.
