Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about buying, financing, and test drives.

What is the return policy? ▾

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all certified pre-owned vehicles. Simply return the vehicle in its original condition and we will process a full refund within 3–5 business days.

How do I book a test drive? ▾

You can book a test drive online by visiting our Test Drive page, selecting your preferred vehicle, date, and contact information. Alternatively, call our hotline at 1-800-AUTOELITE.

Do you offer financing? ▾

Yes, we offer competitive financing options on all vehicles. Use our built-in Loan Calculator on each car's detail page to estimate your monthly payments based on a fixed term of up to 12 months.

What insurance plans are available? ▾

Each vehicle has its own insurance pricing visible on its detail page. We offer Basic Coverage (liability & collision) and Premium Coverage (full comprehensive, roadside assistance, and concierge service). Visit Insurance for more details.

Can I compare two vehicles? ▾

Absolutely! Our Compare Cars tool lets you search for any two vehicles and view a full side-by-side specification comparison in real time.

Do you have upcoming models? ▾

Yes! Visit our Upcoming Models page to see a preview of all 2027 vehicles arriving in our inventory. You can register your interest and be notified at launch.

Where are your dealerships located? ▾

We have locations on the West Coast, Central US, and East Coast. Visit our Dealerships page for addresses, hours, and contact information for each location.
