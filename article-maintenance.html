Top 10 Maintenance Tips for Supercars

April 15, 2026 8 min read By NYH Motors Service Team

Owning a supercar is one of life's great privileges. But with great horsepower comes great responsibility. These machines demand a level of care far beyond an ordinary vehicle. Here are our top 10 expert tips from the NYH Motors service department.

1
Change Engine Oil on Schedule — Never Skip It.
High-performance engines run at extreme tolerances and temperatures. Always use the manufacturer-specified oil grade and change it every 5,000 miles or annually, whichever comes first.
2
Monitor Tyre Pressure Weekly.
Performance tyres are wider and run at higher pressures. Even a small deviation causes measurable changes in handling and tyre wear. Invest in a quality digital pressure gauge.
3
Inspect Brake Pads Every 10,000 Miles.
Performance brake systems generate enormous heat. Carbon-ceramic pads can last 70,000+ miles, but compound brakes wear faster. Never let pads reach the wear indicator before replacing.
4
Warm Up the Engine Before Driving Hard.
Cold oil provides less lubrication. Let the engine warm to operating temperature before pushing the car hard — this alone can double the life of your engine.
5
Use Only Premium Fuel.
High-compression performance engines require 98 RON or higher fuel. Using lower octane fuel causes knock and can permanently damage pistons and valves.
Pro tip: Always check your manufacturer's minimum fuel octane rating. Many supercars specify 98 RON minimum — some require 100 RON for full performance.
6
Keep the Cooling System Healthy.
Flush and replace coolant every two years. Check hose condition annually — cracked or swollen hoses are a sign of imminent failure.
7
Store Properly During Off-Season.
If storing for more than 30 days, use a fuel stabiliser, connect a trickle charger, and store on foam pads to prevent flat-spotting on tyres.
8
Service the Transmission Fluid.
Dual-clutch and manual gearboxes require specialised fluid. Change it every 30,000 miles or after any track use, as heat degrades it rapidly.
9
Protect the Paint Professionally.
Apply ceramic coating or PPF (Paint Protection Film) to preserve the finish. A quality ceramic coat lasts 3–5 years and repels UV damage, bird droppings and road grime.
10
Use a Specialist — Not a Generic Workshop.
Supercars require specialist knowledge and equipment. Always use a certified marque specialist or the manufacturer's own service network.
