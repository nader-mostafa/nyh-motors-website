Maintenance Guide

Watch our comprehensive maintenance overview for your vehicle.

Oil & Fluids

Check engine oil, coolant and brake fluid monthly. EVs: monitor battery coolant quarterly.

Tyre Pressure

Maintain optimal tyre pressure. Rotate tyres every 5,000–7,500 miles for even wear.

Brake System

Inspect brake pads every 10,000 miles. EVs benefit from reduced wear via regenerative braking.

Battery Health

Avoid letting the battery drop below 10% or charge above 90% for daily use to maximise longevity.

Air Filters

Replace cabin and engine air filters every 12,000 miles or annually, whichever comes first.

Seasonal Care

Use season-appropriate fluids and tyres. Store the vehicle in shade to protect paint and battery in heat.
